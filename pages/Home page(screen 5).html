After adequate research and study of the 2005 nigerian forum called Nairaland we the team at nairaland have decided to create a new design that would appeal to a wide range of new users and keep our existing users of aout 3million engaged.

This new designed is aimed at delivering a better user experience, the likes of an easier navigation system, a more friendly user interface , a more vibrant look with the use of colors that are welcoming to the sight, well structured Information architecture, and of course well mapped out design heirarchy.

We have also included a mobile friendly and responsive version of the Nairaland forum as this would help users who would love to read news and happenings in the country ‘on the go’ to achieve that effortlessly. Needless to say we are working on an offline mode for readers with bad internet conection and a cross-platform application so you can install on any device you possess.
